About Us

At ADORA, pets are more than companions — they’re family. Our mission is to bring love, care, and joy into every pet’s life while helping families find their perfect furry friends.

From adoption to essentials, we provide everything your pets need to feel safe, happy, and adored. Together, we’re building a community where pets thrive and homes are filled with love.
